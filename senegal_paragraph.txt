SENEGAL

The countries name comes from Senegal river. It's capital town is Dakar. Senegal shares its borders few countries like Mali, Gambia, and Guinea. It's has quite a number of large cities, Dakar, Grand Dakar and Pikine. The last population count which was done in 2012 was 13.6 million Their currency is CFA franc (XOF). They speak variety of languages like French, Wolof, Sonika.
